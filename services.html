Our Services

Everything you need for smarter daily commuting

Vallo is proudly India's first platform to provide all essential local transportation services within a single app. We bring real-time local transport tracking and community-based ride sharing together in one place, designed specifically for everyday riders.

Service 01

Local Transportation Tracking

Live tracking of buses, autos, magic vans, and all local public transport near you — so you never miss a ride or wait blindly at a stop again.

Real-time vehicle location

See exactly where your bus or auto is right now on a live map. Know when it leaves, how far it is, and when it will arrive at your stop.

Accurate arrival estimates

Get precise ETA countdowns for every nearby vehicle. Plan your departure from home without rushing or arriving too early.

Crowd level indicator

Know how crowded a vehicle is before you board — Low, Medium, or High. Especially useful during peak hours and morning rush.

Arrival alerts

Set a notification for any nearby vehicle. Vallo alerts you when it is 2 minutes away so you can step out right on time.

Full route view

View the complete route of any vehicle on the map — from the starting point to the last stop — so you always know where it is headed.

In-app fare payment

Pay your travel fare directly through Vallo using your wallet credits, UPI, or cash. No need to carry exact change every single day.

How it works

1

Open the map

Vallo automatically detects your location and shows all nearby buses, autos, and magic vans within a 2 km radius in real time.

2

Pick your vehicle

Tap any vehicle marker to see its route, current crowd level, driver details, and the exact time it will reach your nearest stop.

3

Track and board

Follow the vehicle live as it approaches. Hit "Notify Driver" so they know you are waiting, then board and pay seamlessly through the app.

4

Rate and get your receipt

Once you reach your destination, rate the ride and download a digital payment receipt — everything saved in your journey history.

Service 02

GoShare — Community Ride Sharing

A safe, affordable, and community-first ride-sharing service where verified riders offer seats on their daily routes to passengers heading the same way.

Verified community riders

Every rider who offers a GoShare ride is verified through Vallo's KYC process. You always know who is driving before you send a request.

Affordable shared fare

Split the cost of any daily commute. Riders set a fair fare per seat and passengers pay only their share — much cheaper than booking a private cab.

Passenger preference

Riders can set gender preferences for their ride — Any, Female Only, or Male Only — creating a more comfortable and trusted travel experience for everyone.

In-ride group chat

Once a request is accepted, passengers and the rider can message each other inside the app — coordinate pickup points, share notes, and stay connected.

Safe credit-based payments

Payment is processed only after the rider accepts your request — never before. Credits are held securely in your Vallo wallet and transferred only on acceptance.

Live ride navigation

During the ride, both rider and passenger can follow the live route on the map. The rider navigates using Vallo and passengers can track the journey in real time.

How it works — for passengers

1

Browse nearby GoShare rides

The home screen shows all active GoShare rides near you. Filter by destination, departure time, vehicle type, and available seats.

2

Send a ride request

Pick a ride, choose how many seats you need, enter your drop location, and send your request — completely free until the rider accepts.

3

Wait for acceptance

You receive a push notification as soon as the rider accepts your request. Your wallet is charged only at this point — never before.

4

Ride, arrive, and rate

Board at the pickup point, track the journey live, and rate your experience after reaching your destination. Your history and receipt are saved automatically.

How it works — for riders offering a ride

1

Publish your ride

Tell Vallo your route, departure time, available seats, and fare per seat. Add your vehicle details and any note for passengers — then go live in seconds.

2

Review and accept requests

Incoming ride requests appear instantly. Review each passenger's profile, drop location, and seat count — then accept or decline with a single tap.

3

Start and complete the ride

Once you have enough passengers, swipe to start the ride. Navigate using the in-app map, pick up all confirmed passengers, and complete the trip at your destination.

4

Earn credits automatically

After the ride is marked complete, credits from all passengers are transferred directly to your Vallo wallet. Use them for your own rides or recharge needs.
