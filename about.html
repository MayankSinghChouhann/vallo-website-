ABOUT VALLO

Building smarter mobility for everyday India

Every morning, millions of students and working professionals across India stand at roadsides wondering when their bus will arrive, whether their ride will show up, and whether public transport can ever become reliable.


Vallo was born from that exact frustration — and from a belief that technology should serve not only premium cities, but also the everyday commuter travelling through Tier 2 and Tier 3 India.

The Story Behind Vallo

Mayank Singh Chouhan, a student at UPES Dehradun, experienced firsthand what it meant to rely on transportation systems with zero visibility, zero safety assurance, and zero connected infrastructure.

Buses arrived without notice. Shared rides happened through scattered phone calls and WhatsApp groups. Payments were cash-only. And if something went wrong during the journey, there was no intelligent support system to help commuters.

That frustration eventually became a question: why should reliable transportation technology only exist for premium users in metro cities?

That question became Vallo — a smart mobility ecosystem built specifically for everyday commuters across India.

Starting from Dehradun and the Uttarakhand region, Vallo was designed to help students, office workers, local travellers, and communities experience transportation that is smarter, safer, connected, and affordable.

Built Differently

Designed for Real India

Unlike traditional transport startups focused only on premium urban markets, Vallo was created for Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities where transport exists — but technology infrastructure still lags behind.

The platform combines live tracking, digital payments, community ride-sharing, verified networks, and safety systems into one seamless application.

Technology With Purpose

Every feature inside Vallo is designed around one core question:

Will this make tomorrow’s commute safer, cheaper, or more human than today’s?

From GoShare community rides to SOS safety alerts, Vallo focuses on practical innovation that directly improves everyday commuting experiences.

Founder & CEO, Vallo

Mayank Singh Chouhan is the founding mind behind Vallo and a student entrepreneur driven by the mission of transforming local transportation across India.

With hands-on involvement in both product design and technology development, he has led the creation of Vallo’s core systems including GoShare ride-sharing, real-time tracking infrastructure, wallet-based payments, and emergency safety features.

His belief is simple: transportation is not a luxury. It is a right — and it should work for everyone.

Based in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, Mayank is currently focused on expanding Vallo across the Uttarakhand region before scaling nationally.
